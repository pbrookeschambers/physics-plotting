| $a < b$ | `$a < b$` | Less Than |
| $a > b$ | `$a > b$` | Greater Than |
| $a \leq b$ | `$a \leq b$` | Less Than or Equal To |
| $a \geq b$ | `$a \geq b$` | Greater Than or Equal To |
| $\pm$ | `$\pm$` | Plus or Minus |
| $\times$ | `$\times$` | Times |
